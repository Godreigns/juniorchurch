Vision & Mission



Who we are, our vision and our mission

Who We Are

We are a team of God’s servants who minister to God in Spirit, in truth and to young people in the body of Christ. We strive to see God glorified in our lives and in our ministry to the young people whom God has called us to serve.
Our teachers, mentors and coaches love God. They have the heart of Christ and love young people. They are committed to empowering them to fulfil their God given purpose

Vision

To have a vibrant generation of world changers and solution providers for God’s kingdom.

Purpose

To raise a generation of young people with God’s love in their heart, who are empowered to fulfil their life purpose, and be a blessing to humanity.
To build a vibrant Junior Church that will be a haven for young people. A place where young people will find God, good friends, sound instruction and life applications on how to live their best life and positively impact their world.

Classes




These are the age groups we teach

2-5 years old

This are the early years. They are activity driven. Their attention span being so little has to be compensated with teaching styles that include sight, touch and kinestetics. They are exposed to lots of activities and games that are done based on stories of the bible. They also love to sing and dance together.

6-11 years old

This age group prefers to have activities that they can do on their own. The Junior Church focuses on teaching them how to consider others before themeselves. They love hearing biblical and real life stories. and their questions are typically “what happened?”. Their reasoning is sharpened and they basically know most stories in the bible. What they desire to know is “Why?”. They desire to understand the reasoning behind the things that happen in the bible. This class can easily tune off if what is taught does not make sense to them. The teaching method must be able to break bible topics into wyas that are easily understandable by the children. They love to ask questions.



12-18 years old

This class is where self identity of the children begins. They are trying to understand themselves, practicality of God’s word to their life, circumstances, technology and the era that they live in. To do this they need to know “how do i do it?”. They understand the biblical theory, but their age needs a guide that will hold their hands and walk them through practical ways of doing the things they read in the bible one step at a time(without them asking). They seek mentors that they can look up to, talk to about the issues of life and express themselves without being judged. To them, a teaching class is interesting when the teacher is able to get them to express themselves. They love the use of the internet and they often tag teaching class ‘fun' when the platform of the internet is used to back what is being taught. Conventional ways are often tagged “old school”.
